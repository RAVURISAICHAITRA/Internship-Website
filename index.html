What is MDS and anemia?

MDS is a rare blood cancer in which blood cells in the bone marrow do not grow to be healthy red blood cells white blood cells , or platelets- all the vital components of the blood . Not being able to develop healthy red blood cells leads to anemia , which is common with MDS . Anemia has many of the same symptoms as MDS and causes even greater fatigue.


It can be exhausting to live with a condition that makes you feel like you're being held back. If you're caring for a loved one with a condition , it can be equally trying . However , right now , research is underway on an investigational study drug to see if it may alleviate anemic symptoms and better manage your MDS .And you may be able to take part of this study.
